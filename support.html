Slow Day Rescue Support

Quick answers, troubleshooting guidance, and contact information.

Contact support

Email support@slowdayrescue.com.

Planned hours: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:00 PM Eastern Time.

Include the Clover merchant name, a description of the issue, and a screenshot when possible. Never send passwords or full card numbers.

Quick troubleshooting

Dashboard does not load

Refresh the page and open the app from your Clover dashboard.

Sales appear delayed

Wait a few minutes, refresh the dashboard, and confirm the merchant and date.

Cannot generate an offer

Select a business type and confirm the day's sales status has loaded.

Frequently asked questions

Does the app edit payments or orders?

No. The current version uses read-only permissions.

Why did it open without an authorization screen?

An already authorized merchant may be sent directly to the dashboard.

How do I report a bug?

Email the steps, expected behavior, actual behavior, device or browser, and a screenshot.
